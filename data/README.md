# Data Sources

## Megalithic Portal Sites (`megalithic_portal_sites.csv`)

Parsed from KML files downloaded from [The Megalithic Portal](https://www.megalithic.co.uk/).
~61,870 unique archaeological sites across 65+ type categories, primarily covering Europe,
the Mediterranean, and parts of Asia/Africa/Americas.

- **License**: Data is copyright Andy Burnham / The Megalithic Portal. Raw KML files are not
  included in this repository. The CSV contains only derived coordinates and type classifications.
- **Deduplication**: Sites within ~111m (3 decimal places of lat/lon) are merged.

## Supplementary Sites (`supplement_sites.json`)

114 major world archaeological sites not present in the Megalithic Portal database,
covering gaps in South America, Mesopotamia, Iran, South Asia, and Southeast Asia.
Hand-curated with coordinates, dates, types, and regions.

## Merged Dataset (`merged_sites.csv`)

The Portal and Supplement datasets merged with 1km deduplication radius.
Columns: name, lat, lon, type, source.

## Great Circle Coordinates (`circle_coordinates.json`, `circle_coordinates.csv`)

360 points (one per degree of bearing) along the great circle defined by pole
(59.682122°N, 138.646087°W). Generated by `analysis/run_all_tests.py`.

## Pleiades Gazetteer (`pleiades_sites.csv`)

**Not included** (too large, ~35,000 sites). Download from:
https://atlantides.org/downloads/pleiades/dumps/pleiades-places-latest.csv.gz

The analysis script will download and decompress this automatically if not present.
The [Pleiades](https://pleiades.stoa.org/) project is a community-built gazetteer of
ancient places, published under CC BY 3.0.

## UNESCO Cultural Heritage Sites (`unesco_cultural_sites.json`)

871 UNESCO World Heritage cultural sites with coordinates. Used for the 108° angular
separation falsification test.
