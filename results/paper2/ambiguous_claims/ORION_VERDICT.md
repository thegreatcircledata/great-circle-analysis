# Orion Intentionality — Verdict

## Claim
The three Giza pyramids were intentionally arranged to mirror Orion's Belt,
specifically as it appeared at 10,500 BCE.

## Test Results

### A. Random Arrangement Test
| Test | p-value | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Unconstrained random triangles | 0.001500 | Significant |
| Collinearity-matched triangles | 0.007400 | Significant |

The collinearity-matched test is the fair comparison: it asks whether the specific shape
(not just the near-collinearity) matches Orion unusually well.

### B. Alternative Asterism Test
- Tested 3420 star triplets (all combinations of 20 brightest stars)
- Orion Belt rank: **#5** out of 3420
- Top 5 matches:
  1. Arcturus-Regulus-Alhena: 0.000104
  2. Sirius-Spica-Altair: 0.000128
  3. Pollux-Spica-Fomalhaut: 0.000137
  4. Regulus-Procyon-Bellatrix: 0.000167
  5. Sirius-Spica-Fomalhaut: 0.000401

### C. Epoch Sensitivity
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Best matching epoch | 3000 CE (d = 0.000381) |
| At 2560 BCE (construction) | d = 0.000405 |
| At 10,500 BCE (Hancock) | d = 0.000444 |
| Total variation across all epochs | 0.000088 |
| Survey measurement uncertainty | ±0.009437 |
| Variation / uncertainty ratio | 0.0× |

The epoch variation is smaller than or comparable to measurement uncertainty — ALL epochs produce essentially the same match. The 10,500 BCE date claim has no geometric support.

## Verdict: **PARTIALLY SUPPORTED**

The Giza layout does match Orion's Belt significantly better than random plateau arrangements, and Orion ranks highly among tested asterisms. However, the match quality barely varies across epochs, so the claim that 10,500 BCE is a special date is NOT supported by the geometry alone.

### Key Findings
1. **Shape match quality**: Procrustes distance = 0.000385 (lower = better)
2. **vs unconstrained random**: p = 0.001500
3. **vs collinearity-matched random**: p = 0.007400
4. **Orion's rank among all bright star triplets**: #5/3420
5. **Epoch sensitivity**: Variation of 0.000088 across 18,000 years
   (survey uncertainty = 0.009437)
